About Me

Hello, my name is Jason and I'm a professional brand and web designer. I specialize in creating visually appealing designs that help businesses stand out in their respective markets. My goal is to work with organizations of all sizes and industries to develop branding and web design solutions that effectively communicate their unique value propositions and help them achieve their business objectives.

To achieve this, I follow a tactical design process that involves understanding your organization's goals, researching your industry and competitors, and collaborating with you to develop a custom design strategy. I ensure that every design I create is not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional, user-friendly, and optimized for search engines.

Design isn't just pretty imagery. It's a tactical application of visual communication to help you achieve your goals. In today's fast-paced and ever-evolving business landscape, having a strong brand and online presence is essential to competing in your market and attracting potential customers.

Let's work together to elevate your brand and web design and achieve your business goals.



Strategy

With your deep understanding of your business, nobody knows it better than you. Through a fun and engaging 3-part strategy session, we can work together to uncover ways to align your unique brand voice, user needs, and business goals. This collaborative approach removes the guesswork and leads to better-targeted solutions. By actively listening to and involving my clients in the process, they feel empowered and intelligent, resulting in a successful partnership.

Design

Design is not just about aesthetics; it is the solution to a problem within a specific set of limitations. To achieve your goals, it is essential to understand your users' expectations and the tangible results you require. Design must be informed by research and understanding to deliver better-targeted solutions. Whether you need a poster, catalog, website, or ad, rest assured that your project is in capable hands.

Development

In the traditional design process, a designer would create a visual design and then hand it off to a developer for implementation. This process can lead to long email chains, multiple revisions, and a general sense of fatigue for everyone involved. However, as an all-in-one design expert, I can eliminate these bottlenecks and save clients time and money. In the rare instance when another developer is needed, I have the expertise to communicate effectively with them and keep projects on track. With me, you can trust that your design projects will be streamlined and efficient, from start to finish.
